Disaster, and a Jam and Cheese Sandwich

Source: u/oh_no551

Description

A standard jam and cheese sandwich, and staple of my childhood.

Ingredients and Steps

  • Bread of choice
  • Jam of choice
  • Sliced Mozzarella cheese.
  • Kitchen countertop
  • Butter knife
  • Sharp knife
  1. Place bread on countertop, make sure to leave bread loaf packaging open to make the remaining bread stale faster.
  2. Slice cheese on countertop, carefully avoiding fingers - 95% success rate.
  3. Place cheese on a slice of bread.
  4. Spread jam of choice on cheese and bread.
  5. Press the two slices of bread together and walk away, allowing any jam that misse the slice of bread dry on the counter top until the two fuse at the molecular level.
